XXX The External, Internal, Internal then External and External then Internal radio buttons, are used to specify the blending method.

The difference between the later two options is only apparent when flats become very short after the first blend is applied.

If the second blend would produce a flat smaller than the Minimum Line value, the second blend will fail.

Depending on which option is selected, this could be an internal or external corner.
