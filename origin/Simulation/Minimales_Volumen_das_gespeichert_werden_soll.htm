Value sets the minimum volume of cut that is saved.

The volume of cut is saved so that when you go backwards and forwards through the simulation, it shows the most accurate removal of material.

If this value was set to a much larger value, as the user goes back through the simulation the material would not be drawn back where I should be.

The larger the value, the faster the simulation is.
